Welcome to Imani Coffee Ltd

Imani Coffee Ltd bridges Kenyan producers like Namba Coffee Estate with roasters around the globe. We source, cup, and grade the finest coffees from across Kenya and manage the export logistics to ensure freshness and traceability.

About Us

Imani Coffee Ltd is a licensed coffee exporter based in Nairobi, Kenya. Our mission is to connect smallholder farmers and estates with importers and roasters worldwide. Our experienced team comes from backgrounds in agronomy, coffee trading, and logistics, bringing decades of expertise to every shipment.

Our Coffees / Origins

We offer coffees from Kenya’s renowned regions, each with distinct cup profiles:

  • Namba (Kiambu): Bright citrus and blackberry notes; SL28 and SL34 varieties.
  • Kisii: Floral aromas with stone fruit flavours; Ruiru 11 and Batian varieties.
  • Nyeri: Winey acidity and red currant flavours; SL28 and SL34 varieties.
  • Kirinyaga: Complex sweetness with notes of blackcurrant and caramel; SL28 and SL34.

Contact us for detailed spec sheets and sample requests.

How We Work

Imani Coffee Ltd partners directly with estates and cooperatives. We cup and grade each lot to determine quality, then manage export logistics including milling, documentation, and shipping. Transparency and traceability are at the heart of our process, allowing buyers to follow their coffee from farm to cup.

Export Services

For importers and roasters we offer:

  • Sample preparation and cupping notes.
  • Full export logistics including documentation (Coffee Directorate, KEBS, KRA compliance).
  • Flexible shipping options and minimum order quantities.

Sustainability & Traceability

We believe in responsible trade. We support initiatives that empower farmers, promote gender inclusion, and protect the environment. Our supply chain is fully traceable, giving you confidence in the ethics and quality behind every bag.
